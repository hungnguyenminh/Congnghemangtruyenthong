    FAQ ABOUT THE TRIP

    Is an International License valid in Vietnam?

    “Can I ride a motorbike legally in Vietnam with my international driving license or with my home country license? ”could be the most popular question and it’s still the hardest question to answer because it’s hard to find the official information from local authority or any office website of the government. From the latest updates, the regulations for foreign tourists riding motorbikes legally in Vietnam with an international driving license were not consistent throughout the country. The requirements varied by region and were subject to change. Generally, Northern Vietnam authorities allow foreign tourists to ride motorbikes with an International Driving Permit (IDP) in addition to their valid home country driver’s license, while other regions require a Vietnamese motorbike license to ride legally in Vietnam. However, after over 10 years of riding motorcycle tours in Vietnam, we can tell the good news is police and local authorities are quite friendly with tourists so if you can ride a motorbike well and carefully after the local tour guide, no police will stop or care about your license. Especially with a valid international license, and if you don’t ride over the speed limit…you can ride to regions in Vietnam So the answer to the question “Is international license valid in Vietnam” is YES. The ONLY problem is the INSURANCE, many travel insurance companies don’t cover this kind of trip if tourists don’t have Vietnamese motorbike licenses.

    The biggest insurance companies in Vietnam are Bao Viet( National Insurance Company) and Gras Savoye Willis (French Insurance Broker). They are big in Vietnam but they won’t cover you if you have no Vietnamese motorbike riding license. To get a Vietnamese motorcycle license or convert your license to a Vietnamese one is complicated and takes plenty of time to do paperwork and wait. Therefore, the suggestion is that you’d better buy insurance in your home country before coming on tour by motorbike in Vietnam.
